
    Title: OS Open Zoomstack

    Link to product: https://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/products/os-open-zoomstack

    Price: Price: Free for everyone


    Description: 

    Ordnance Survey's OS Open Zoomstack is an open vector basemap showing coverage of Great Britain from a national level, right down to street detail.


    

    Use cases: <ul>
<li>Use as a basemap in GIS, interactive web maps or mobile apps</li>
<li>Integrate your own data to give it geographic context</li>
<li>Customise the style and content to make your perfect map</li>
</ul>
