
    Title: OS Open Linked Identifiers

    Link to product: https://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/products/os-open-linked-identifiers

    Price: Price: Free for everyone


    Description: 

    OS Open Linked Identifiers provides authoritative relationship between UPRNs, USRNs, TOIDs and meta data to enable reliable matching; for greater data connectivity.


    

    Use cases: <p>The OS Open Linked Identifiers product provides the valuable relationships between the following features found in OS MasterMap products (TopographicAreas limited to address and road features):</p>
<ul>
<li>TopographicArea* (TOID): OS MasterMap Topographic Layer</li>
<li>RoadLink(TOID): OS MasterMap Highways Networks &ndash; Roads</li>
<li>Road(TOID): OS MasterMap Highways Networks &ndash; Roads</li>
<li>ORRoadLink/ORRoadNode(GUID): OS Open Roads</li>
<li>Street(USRN): OS MasterMap Highways Networks &ndash; Roads</li>
<li>BLPU(UPRN): AddressBase Premium</li>
</ul>
<p><sup>*restricted to address and road features.</sup></p>
