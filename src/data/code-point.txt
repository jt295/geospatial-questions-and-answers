
    Title: Code-Point

    Link to product: https://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/products/code-point

    

    Description: 

    Code-Point is a postcode locator with postcode units in Great Britain and Northern Ireland. Get started with geographical analysis, simple route planning and asset management using Code-Point.


    

    Use cases: <p><strong>Code-Point data provides:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>information about the number and type of postal delivery points in the postcode;</li>
<li>a positional quality indicator (PQI), indicating the quality of the data underlying the Code-Point location coordinate (CPLC);</li>
<li>the country indicator (either England, Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland);</li>
<li>the postcode type;</li>
<li>the National Health Service region and area codes; and</li>
<li>the local government county, district and ward codes.</li>
</ul>
